Wises Foot Co. (Walk In Someone Else's Shoes!) is all about sustainable walking. Shoes are abundently availaibe, yet unused in closets across America. We strive put every sole available to work, thereby eliminating unnecissary waste, and enable a more efficient consumer to try before they buy. Shoes are expensive, comfort should not.
